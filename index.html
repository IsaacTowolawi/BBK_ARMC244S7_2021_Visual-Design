BLEACH

The goal was to create an interactive magazine based on the popular Anime series BLEACH. Fitting the theme of most of my assignments i wanted to introduce people to popular anime series of the 21st century. I looked to BLEACH forums that I would constantly use for inspiration. I initially wanted to develop an interactive multimedia player on mobile, which would display the most popular anime scenes in video form but, opted for a magazine due to its simplicity and the somewhat impact it could have on specific readers who want to get into anime. This magazine is meant to be a casual introduction to the popular series and information about specific characters in the show.
