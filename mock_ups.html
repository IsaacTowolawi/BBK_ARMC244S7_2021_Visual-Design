MOCK UP

  • For this website I wanted to create an interactive online magazine that will Assist new readers with the 2000s anime Bleach.
  • This is also an info-mag essentially discussing each characters history; where they are & where they are going within the anime series.
  • I wanted a minimalistic appearance in the magazine and I decided to have clear spaces to convey a premium/ minimalistic feel.
  • There are 4 characters in total at different stages within the series.
  • To commence with the premium minimalistic feel i opted to use the “COM4t Drify” typeface as it was similar to “Seingalt” typeface which was mostly used in high fashion magazine.
  • I also used the Gotham typeface due to its boldness and it allowing me to display all-caps effectively. The Gotham typeface is one of the most popular type used to date.
  • These mockups were created on both In-Design and Photoshop but mostly In-Design. I created a print preset of 21cm x 29cm > Facing Pages > Pages 2 > Start# > Columns 4 > Columns 0.42.33cm. On the Margin mode i change it to 3cm & Bleeding and Slug 0.3cm.
